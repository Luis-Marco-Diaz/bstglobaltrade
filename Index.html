Mission

BST has the purpose of providing the procedures, the practical tools, a centralized link to the consolidation via the Internet to generate commercial globalization and communication between manufactures, wholesale companies, importers, exporters, factory representatives, etc., dedicated to the marketing of Bearings, Heavy-Duty Truck, Buses and Trailer parts, Agricultural, Construction and Mining Industries.

Vision

To create the infrastructure for companies to develop new automated and centralized commercial alternatives in related fields in the global market, through the most up-to-date, dynamic and outstanding means of communication, software and administration for the Internet.
